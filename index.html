ABOUT THE GROUP

N.M. Industries was started in 2011 at Hosur, one of the major industrial zones in Tamil Nadu and very near to Bangalore, India. N.M.Industries is an ISO 9001:2015 certified and company-wide TQM practice initiated industry that specializes in automobile components manufacturing. Ours is a 7200 square feet industrial building with a high roof shed built on one acre land. We are meeting 100% customers requirements with backup power. Driven by well qualified engineers, supervisors and skilled employees, we produce and supply main stand tubular components, welded auto components and stamped parts to our customers. We also have capabilities to inspect all the products to ensure 100% quality products delivered in a timely and cost efficient manner.

Our Valued Customers

Neel Metal Products Limited

Neel Metal Products Limited (NMPL) engages in the manufacture and sale of automotive components for TVS motors and Honda motors in India. The company offers various finished automotive components, including wheels rims for motorcycles; exhaust systems and welded steel tubes for two-wheelers; complete frames for scooters; and stamped components, and complete fabricated and painted body for three-wheelers.

Indo Autotech Limited (IAL) are the manufacturers and suppliers of precision sheet metal components, tubular parts and assemblies for Honda Motors.
