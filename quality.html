We are here to deliver the best quality products. Our PPM are maintained well within the customer requirements and our delivery performances are always 100%. One of the top priorities in our manufacturing industry is protecting the employees from safety hazards.

Quality

  1. Top 5 objectives in our quality system:
  2. 1. Increase customer satisfaction - Provide them with high quality products consistently to maintain our industry standards.
  3. 2. Reduce customer rejection - Introduced Poka yoke system (mistake proofing) to prevent the defects.
  4. 3. Reduce internal rejection - Introduced Kaizen practice (process improvement) which helps us for next process owner.
  5. 4. Reduce supplier rejection - Supplier training is given and supplier audit helps us to reduce the supplier rejection.
  6. 5. Increase number of kaizen - Continous training and motivation is given to the employees, which helps us to get more suggestions and kaizens.

Safety

  1. Top safety measures we take care in our industry:
  2. 1. Machine Guarding - Sensor and double hand operating switches are inbuilt in the machine for employees safety.
  3. 2. Electrical - Training is provided to employees for electrical safety practises and standards.
  4. 3. Lockout/Tagout - Lockout/Tagout system is followed that helps us to minimize the risk of accidents and machine breakdowns.
  5. 4. Maintenance - Periodic maintenance checks are performed to minimize the risk of accidents.

TQM Practice

We follow TQM as it is the management system for a customer-focused organization that involves all employees in continual improvement. It uses strategy, data, and effective communications to integrate the quality discipline into the culture and activities of the organization. Many of these concepts are present in modern quality management systems. We follow about 22 methods in TQM and 10 methods through system.

Sunrise Meetings

The Sunrise Meeting helps us to re-boot our business each and every day, allowing our team to focus on the business' objectives. It is one way of improving productivity and performance through effective habits and routines. It is all about colloborating with the team and planning ahead for the day. It also gives an opportunity for the employees to give ideas for continous improvement.

Gemba Corner

Gemba Corner is a Japanese term meaning 'the work place' where the leaders and employees can see the state of the process for themselves. We leaders, managers and supervisors are expected to simply observe and understand process. As part of the Kaizen methodology (continous improvement), it is also supposed to encourage greater communication, transparency and trust between the employees and leadership.
